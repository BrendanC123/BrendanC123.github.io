Creating the AI

This AI was created to replicate the systems used by Creative Assembly in Alien: Isolation. In order to do this, I first created a behaviour tree system in Unity that I could then use as a base to develop the AI in the game.

The monster is blind and so can only rely on their hearing to hunt down the player. The dilemma is that the player is also blind and needs to create sound in order to see their surroundings. In trying to mirror the Alien: Isolation’s AI the AI doesn’t cheat and has to rely solely on noises heard to find and hunt the player. The AI starts roaming being told by the director where on the map to investigate (the director AI was created by another team member). Whenever the AI hears a sound it investigates it, slowly circling its position. There is a priority system in place to help the player, if the heard sound is an object thrown by the player, the AI will favor that for a few seconds so that if the player steps just after throwing a distraction the AI will still go to the distraction. If it gets close enough to the player, it identifies that it is something it should hunt and begins to chase the player. The monster then runs after the player and won’t lose track of them until the player closes a door on the monster at which point the monster has to locate and hunt the player all over again.
